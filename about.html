What inspires me.

I am a huge fan of arty, noisy, experimental sounds. I like to figure out how to make strange stuff interesting. Conversely I love to experiment with repetition, finding the little hooks and motifs that build a commonality, all those captivating riffs that hold onto familiarity as the sound world evolves. Some of my favourite composers are Eno, Dallwitz, Newman, Desplat, Wendy Carlos (to name a few).

What's in the record box?

Of the last few years – My most played albums would most likely be Parquet Courts' Sunbathing Animal, I fell in love with this band immediately, and this album in my opinion really captures their early sound perfectly. King Gizzard and the Lizard Wizard's Sketches of Brunswick East, a fantastic collab between Gizzard and Mild High Club, every song is a testament to their incredible songwriting. Otoboke Beaver's Itekoma Hits just a manic, adrenaline ride of Japanese design!

Of the last ten years – Yo La Tengo's May I Sing With Me, although much older I think it’s one of my most played albums of most years. Foxygen's We Are The 21st Century Ambassadors of Peace and Magic, a great pastiche of the rock and roll sound with a sense of self indulgence. Ty Segall's Emotional Mugger, really could pick any album by Ty but this is on my most played most likely.

Of ever – Some big honourable mentions of yesteryears that I can never get enough of: Beck - Stereopathetic Soulmanure, Kinks - Kinda Kinks, The Velvet Underground - White Light/ White Heat, Can - Delay, Shonen Knife - Burning Farm, The Clash - London Calling, The Doors - Strange Days, ESG - Come Away With ESG; (and so on…)

That's me in a nutshell!
