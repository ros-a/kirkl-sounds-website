I'm Kirkl

A while ago I studied composition and sound design at Kingston Uni, quite some time before that I was sold a shabby guitar at a car boot sale for £10; somewhere in between I got good enough to make it my life; and now I sell my labour to the highest bidder. So here I am! Composer/sound designer for hire!

Music – I often make use of software synths and organs, but nothing completes a piece for me quite like the sound of live recorded instruments. I especially like to play around with guitars, keys, vocals, analogue synths – and anything odd and unique that I can get a hold of.

Sound design – I am a big fan of ambient noise and drones. I often like to blend some foley and field recordings with digitally crafted sounds, either to compliment or juxtapose the visuals.
